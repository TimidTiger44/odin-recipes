Chicken Fajitas

Description

This simply, yet delicious combination of meat and veggies will satisfy any dinner craving. Mild, yet powerful seasoning is used to enhance the flavor of the chicken and veggies; which combine together over rice or inside a tortilla to give a wonderful and tasty experience.

Ingredients

  • chicken breasts - 1 lb
  • red bell pepper (sliced) - 1
  • yellow bell pepper (sliced) - 1
  • spices
  • olive oil - 1 tsp
  • tortillas or rice (optional)

Steps

  1. Season chicken
  2. Heat olive oil over medium heat in skillet
  3. Cook chicken for 7 minutes on each side, turning frequently to avoid burning.
  4. Remove chicken from skillet and sear veggies
  5. Add chicken and veggies to rice or tortillas
  6. Add condiments to taste
