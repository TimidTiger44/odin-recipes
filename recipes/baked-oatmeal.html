Baked Oatmeal

Description

This warm and smooth breakfast favorite can be enjoyed any time of the day and even heated up for a quick and filling snack.

Ingredients

  • Quick or rolled oats - 1 cup
  • choice of milk or milk substitute - 1 cup
  • vanilla 1 tsp
  • chocolate chips - 1 tbsp (15g)
  • banana - 1/2 medium (mashed)

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a oven safe bowl
  2. Bake ingredients for 30 minutes at 400 degrees F
