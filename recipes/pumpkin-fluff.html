Pumpkin Fluff

Description

Simple and tasty fall favorite. Can be combined with choice of graham crackers or fruit(s). Great for any tailgate or carry-in.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cool whip
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin
  • 1 tsp pumpkin spice
  • 1 tbsp marshmellow fluff
  • 1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
  • milk added until smooth (~1/2 cup)

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients except milk
  2. Add milk and stir mixture until consistency is smooth.
  3. Refrigerate at least 3 hours.
